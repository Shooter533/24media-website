## 1. Business Overview

**24Media** is an AI automation agency based in Australia that installs done-for-you AI systems into small businesses — specifically in trades, home services, health & wellness, and legal.

The core proposition is simple: **small businesses are losing money every time their phone goes unanswered or a lead sits cold for more than 5 minutes.** 24Media fixes that with AI agents that respond instantly, 24/7, without adding headcount.

This is not a software product. It is a **managed service** — we build, configure, and maintain the system for each client. They get results without touching the technology.

## 3. Core Services

### 🎙️ AI Voice Receptionist
**The flagship product.**

An AI agent answers every inbound call — 24/7, no hold music, no voicemail. It handles common queries, qualifies the caller, and books appointments directly into the calendar. If escalation is needed, it transfers to a human or sends an SMS follow-up.

- Always-on: nights, weekends, public holidays
- Books directly into Google Calendar
- Sounds natural — not a phone tree
- Call logs and transcripts delivered to the business owner

**Who it's for:** Any business that takes bookings or quote requests by phone.

---

### ⚡ Speed-to-Lead
**Turn web enquiries into booked jobs in under 60 seconds.**

When a prospect fills in a contact form or enquiry, the system fires an instant automated response — SMS and/or voice call — within seconds. Speed-to-lead is the #1 factor in conversion; first to respond wins the job.

- Triggers from website forms, Facebook Lead Ads, Google Ads leads
- Instant SMS acknowledgement + follow-up call from AI agent
- Books appointment or escalates to human

**Who it's for:** Businesses running any form of paid advertising or web enquiry.

---

### 📱 Missed Call Text-Back
**Every missed call gets an instant SMS reply.**

When a call is missed (on the tools, in a meeting, after hours), an automatic text fires within seconds: "Hi, we missed your call — here's how to book or reply to this message." Keeps leads warm before they call a competitor.

- Fires within seconds of a missed call
- Personalised message with business name
- Two-way SMS conversation handled by AI

**Who it's for:** Any business that misses calls. Entry-level product, easy sell.

- **Direct and plain-spoken** — no buzzwords, no tech jargon
- Confident, not arrogant
- Empathetic to the business owner's reality (busy, sceptical, time-poor)
- Occasionally use the customer's language: "on the tools," "quote requests," "jobs"
- Australian English throughout

### Brand Positioning
- **Not a tech startup** — we are a service business that happens to use advanced tech
- **Not enterprise** — we work with small business owners, not corporate procurement
- **Outcome-focused** — leads captured, calls answered, bookings made

## 8. Visual Style Direction

### Aesthetic
- **Modern, clean, conversion-optimised** — not flashy, not cluttered
- Think: agency credibility meets small-business approachability
- Dark mode hero section with bright accent (electric blue, lime green, or orange) — bold, tech-forward but not cold
- Light sections below fold for readability

### Colour Palette Suggestion
- **Primary background (hero):** Deep navy `#0A0F1E` or near-black `#0D0D0D`
- **Accent:** Electric blue `#3B82F6` or bright teal `#14B8A6`
- **Secondary accent (CTAs):** Vivid orange `#F97316` or lime `#84CC16`
- **Body text sections:** Off-white `#F9FAFB` background, dark text
- Keep it bold and high-contrast

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Geometric sans-serif — Inter, Geist, or Plus Jakarta Sans (bold weight)
- **Body:** Inter or system-ui — clean, legible
- Large, confident type in the hero — not small and timid

### Imagery / Illustration Style
- Avoid generic stock photos of people at computers
- Use: abstract AI/wave visualisations, phone call animations, real trade business imagery (plumber, electrician in context)
- If using illustrations: clean line-art or Lottie animations showing the "call → book" flow
- Consider a live demo widget or audio waveform animation in the hero

### Component Feel
- Rounded cards (8–12px radius)
- Subtle glassmorphism or gradient borders on feature cards
- Generous whitespace — premium feel, not cramped
- Sticky header with CTA button always visible

## 9. Key Messaging Framework

### Hero Copy Direction
```
Headline: "Never Miss Another Job."
Subhead: "24Media installs AI receptionists and lead response systems for 
          trade businesses — so every call gets answered and every lead 
          gets followed up, automatically."
CTA: "Book a Free Demo" | Secondary: "See How It Works"
```

### The Problem Block
```
Heading: "You're losing jobs you don't even know about."
Body: Every missed call, every slow reply, every voicemail left on read 
      is a job that went to someone else. The average small business 
      misses 40% of inbound calls. The first to respond wins 78% of jobs.
      Your competition is already using this.
```

### How It Works (3 Steps)
1. **We build your AI system** — customised to your business, your services, your tone. Takes about a week.
2. **It goes live on your number** — customers call, the AI answers. Leads come in, it follows up instantly.
3. **You just get the bookings** — we handle the tech, you run the business.

### Objection-Busting FAQ
- *"Will it sound like a robot?"* — No. Modern AI voice sounds natural. Callers often don't know.
- *"What if someone needs to speak to a real person?"* — It transfers the call or sends a message to you immediately.
- *"How long does setup take?"* — Most clients are live within 5–7 business days.
- *"Do I need to change my phone number?"* — No. We route through your existing number.
- *"What's the contract?"* — Month-to-month after setup. No lock-in.
- *"Is this just a chatbot?"* — No. This is a voice AI that answers phone calls and takes action — books appointments, captures info, and responds to SMS.
